About LovEat

LovEat helps you discover the best restaurants wherever you are. Describe what you're craving in your own words, like "spicy seafood with an ocean view" or "cozy place for a date night", and get matched with restaurants that truly fit, based on real menus and reviews.

You can also snap a photo of any restaurant to instantly explore its menu. No searching, no guessing. Just point your camera and see what's on offer.

LovEat is free to use with generous monthly limits. Whether you're a tourist exploring a new city or a local looking for something different, LovEat is your personal dining guide.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address from the sign-in provider. This is used to create and manage your LovEat account.

Location data. When you grant permission, we access your device's GPS coordinates to find nearby restaurants and to identify restaurants when using the menu scanner. Location data is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers.

Search queries. When you search for restaurants, your query is sent to our servers to return results. Queries are processed in real time and are not linked to your identity.

Camera and photos. The menu scanner requires access to your device's camera. When you take a photo, it is sent to our servers to identify the restaurant. Photos are processed in real time and are not stored after the request is complete.

Device storage. Recent searches, location preferences, and authentication tokens are stored locally on your device. This data is never sent to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and manage your monthly usage limits.
  • To return relevant restaurant search results based on your query and location.
  • To identify restaurants from photos you take with the menu scanner.
  • To process subscription purchases and manage your account tier.

Third-Party Services

LovEat integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign-In / Google Sign-In: Used for account authentication. Your name and email are received from the provider you choose. Their respective privacy policies apply.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat receives your anonymous user ID and purchase status to process transactions. RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.
  • We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

Data Retention

Your account data (name, email, usage history) is retained for as long as your account exists. When you request account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers.

Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing support.loveatapp@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. Please note that deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. You must cancel your subscription through your device's App Store settings before requesting account deletion.

Children's Privacy

LovEat is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support.loveatapp@gmail.com.
